## About Me: System Design & Agentic AI

I engineer **end-to-end AI systems** with a focus on **Agentic Architectures** and **System Design**. My expertise spans:

* **Agentic AI & LLMOps**: Designing, building, and orchestrating complex multi-agent workflows (LangGraph, CrewAI, ADK).
* **System Backend**: Integrating graph databases (Neo4j) and vector stores (FAISS, ChromaDB) for efficient, structured knowledge retrieval and high-precision data mapping.
* **Deep Learning & MLOps**: Translating complex deep learning models (PyTorch/TensorFlow) into reliable, scalable services using cloud-native tools (Docker, K8s, CI/CD).

## How I Build Production-Grade Systems

My engineering philosophy prioritizes robustness, scalability, and technical depth:

1.  **Architecture**: Adopting a **modular, microservices-based design** utilizing asynchronous workers, queues, and specialized vector/graph stores (FAISS, Neo4j, ChromaDB).
2.  **Deployment**: Ensuring platform stability and portability with **Cloud-Native Deployment** standards, primarily using **Docker and Kubernetes** for reproducible AI services.
3.  **Performance**: Prioritizing **Observability** and implementing efficient **caching layers** and **FastAPI** endpoints to meet strict performance and reliability SLAs.
